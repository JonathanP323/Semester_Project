Directions

From downtown Newark, head north on Broad Street, then turn right onto Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd. Our restaurant is located within the NJIT campus.

Parking Information

Parking is available at the NJIT Parking Deck, located at 154 Summit Street. Additional street parking is available in the surrounding area.

Public Transportation

NJIT is accessible via the Newark Light Rail, with stops at Warren Street/NJIT and Norfolk Street stations. Several NJ Transit bus routes also serve the area.
